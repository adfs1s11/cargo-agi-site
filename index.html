Our Vision

Our vision is to provide an easy-to-use, scalable tool that reduces manual work, improves visibility, and helps you run your business more efficiently – without the high cost or complexity of large enterprise systems.

Solving Your Daily Challenges

  • Too much time spent on manual data entry.
  • Lack of real-time information on your deliveries and fleet.
  • Difficulty getting useful data for planning or maintenance.
  • High costs and steep learning curves of existing software.

Our Phased Approach

1. Start Simple (MVP)

  • Easy Data Import: Upload your delivery and order data directly from Excel (.xlsx, .csv).
  • Basic Online View: See all your data clearly in simple web lists and tables.
  • Benefit: Instantly reduce manual data entry time and get digital visibility with minimal effort.

2. Improve Operations (v1)

  • Web-Based Dispatch: Create, update, and manage your delivery assignments online.
  • Driver Mobile App: Drivers receive assignments on a mobile app and send real-time status updates.
  • Basic Document Scan: Use simple OCR to capture key information from delivery documents.
  • Benefit: Streamline dispatch, get real-time updates, and reduce paper handling.

3. Add Value (v2-Lite)

  • Maintenance Alerts: Set reminders for vehicle maintenance by time or distance, and log completion.
  • Benefit: Keep vehicles running smoothly and reduce unexpected repair costs.
